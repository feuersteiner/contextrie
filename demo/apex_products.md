# Apex Cloud Q3 2025 Product Announcements

**Press Release — October 15, 2025**

Apex Cloud Infrastructure today announced several new product offerings at its annual ApexConnect developer conference in Austin, Texas.

**New Services:**

- **ApexAI Studio**: Managed GPU clusters for ML training, available in 3 regions
- **ApexEdge**: Edge computing nodes for IoT and low-latency applications
- **ApexVault**: Zero-knowledge encryption key management with HSM backing

**Partnerships:** New integrations with Snowflake, Databricks, and MongoDB Atlas.

**Sustainability:** Apex commits to 100% renewable energy across all data centers by 2027. Virginia facility now solar-powered.

**Pricing:** ApexAI Studio starts at $2.40/GPU-hour. Early adopters receive 20% discount through Q1 2026.

CEO Michael Chen: "We're doubling down on AI infrastructure and edge computing—the future of cloud is distributed, intelligent, and sustainable."
